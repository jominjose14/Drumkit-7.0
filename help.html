Drum Kit 7.0



Best viewed on a 15" display


This drumkit can be played in two ways:

  • Mouse/Touch
  • Keyboard


Mouse-Clicks/Touch

In the first case, click or touch on the letter-buttons located next to each drum part to play it. Its as simple as that! Make sure you enable multi-touch in case of a touch-screen.


Keyboard

In the second case, before using the keyboard to play, check out the [Controls] page to understand the default key-bindings.

Edit the controls and click 'Update Changes' to register your controls. Or, choose one of the two pre-defined Control Schemes: Easy or Pro.

Press the linked up key on the keyboard to play the corresponding drum-part. Enjoy!


About

This is a free online drumkit web app, wired up using Javascript. Audio samples have been collected from sites like findsounds.com and samplefocus.com, and edited to suit the drumkit's feel and appeal.
